If, as expected, out-of-favour midfielder Mesut Ozil is left out of Arsenal's finalised 25-man Premier League squad later today, where could the 32-year-old move to? Well, the German's options are fairly limited, with every transfer window in Europe now shut, apart from in Portugal, where clubs have until October 25 to sign new players. The Russian window closed on October 17, so what about a move to either the Chinese Super League or the UAE? Well, the CSL's third transfer window shut at end of September, while the window in the UAE closed on October 6, although their FA are still awaiting Fifa approval to reopen the window by a month. However, Ozil could still make a switch to Qatar, whose window closes on October 25, or the MLS, whose secondary window does not shut until October 29.
